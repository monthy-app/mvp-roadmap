# E8: demo-ideas skill — write-up

> Completed: 2026-07-16 · Written: 2026-07-16 · Append-only history; not linted.

## What shipped

`/demo-ideas`: 2–3 demos of the product's current state, grounded in ticked reality and verified before proposed — exact runnable steps, expected output, time-to-run. Artifact proof above the MVP line, feedback bait below it, and an offer to script the winner.

## Evidence

Commit in the `8c46b26` push; CI green; registered as a slash command and installed by the five-skill global install.

## What surprised

The hard rule turned out to be "verify before proposing" — the demo that fails in front of its audience is always the one nobody ran first. The skill spends most of its words on that discipline rather than on demo formats.
